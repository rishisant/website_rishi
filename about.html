About Me
Who is this guy?


My name's Rishi. Duh! But let me tell you more about my journey here.

I was born in sunny Palm Springs, California. I grew up there for a few years before
my parents packed their bags and moved out. I've called Texas my home ever since,
living in San Antonio and the Greater Houston area.

The first PC that I ever coded on was my Dad's old Windows XP computer. He showed me
the possibilities that come with computers and although he was never too proficient in
them, he let me discover the world of computers through video games, graphic design,
audio engineering, and of course, programming.

I spent a good portion of my childhood coding video games and small projects,
designing fonts, graphics, code, and much much more. It was an incredibly
gratifying experience, considering that I had so much more free-time to indulge
in my personal projects.

Nowadays, outside of school, I try to work on one large project every semester. I seriously
enjoy coding and problem solving, but outside of that, I have a wide variety of interests.

I enjoy playing basketball, lifting weights, learning languages, and reading in my free
time.

I'm currently reading Demon-Haunted World by Carl Sagan.
